关于我们

Where Elegance Meets Everyday Beauty

优雅与日常之美相遇的地方

At Petite Fleur SG, we believe that flowers have the power to transform any moment into something extraordinary. Our small but passionate team carefully selects the freshest blooms and designs each bouquet with intention and artistry.

在 Petite Fleur SG，我们相信鲜花有力量将每一个平凡的时刻变得不平凡。我们的小团队精心挑选最新鲜的花材，以匠心和艺术感设计每一束花。

Whether it's a birthday celebration, a quiet "I'm thinking of you," or a grand anniversary dinner, we're here to help you say it with flowers.

无论是生日庆典、一句温暖的"我在想你"，还是浪漫的结婚纪念日晚餐，我们都希望帮你用花来表达心意。
